## üé¨ Photography Guidelines

### General Best Practices:

#### ‚úÖ DO:
- Use natural lighting when possible
- Show diverse student representation
- Capture authentic moments
- Use high-resolution original images
- Maintain consistent color grading
- Show modern, clean environments
- Include branding subtly where appropriate
- Get model releases for identifiable people

#### ‚ùå DON'T:
- Use stock photos that look generic
- Over-edit or heavily filter images
- Use low-resolution images
- Include copyrighted materials
- Show outdated equipment or facilities
- Use inconsistent photography styles
- Include cluttered or messy backgrounds
- Show identifiable people without consent
