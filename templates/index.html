About University of Penasia

盈亞大學（University of Penasia - Penasia School of Continuing Education）是香港首間結合嚴謹商業管理訓練與專業餐飲及酒店專才的高等院校。

As the only Hong Kong institution delivering Pearson BTEC Higher National Diplomas with integrated culinary management, we prepare graduates for leadership roles in global hospitality businesses, restaurant entrepreneurship, and hotel management.

With Education Code No. 25291638, we offer professional development opportunities in hospitality, business management, and culinary arts with programs designed to meet industry standards.
