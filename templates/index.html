About PenAsia

盈亞持續教育中心 (PenAsia Continuing Education Centre) is a licensed educational institution committed to providing quality continuing education programs in Hong Kong.

With Education License No. 593958, we offer professional development opportunities in hospitality, business management, and culinary arts with programs designed to meet industry standards.
