Entry Requirements

Diploma Programs

Hotel Culinary Management Diploma
  • Form 6 graduation or equivalent
  • IELTS 4.5 or equivalent English proficiency
  • Age 18 or above
  • Passion for culinary arts
BTEC Business Management
  • HKDSE with minimum grades
  • IELTS 5.5 or equivalent
  • Age 18 or above
  • Interest in business management

Certificate Programs

Western Bakery & Pastry
  • Form 3 graduation or equivalent
  • Age 18 or above
  • Basic Cantonese understanding
  • Physical ability to stand for extended periods
Western Cuisine
  • Form 3 graduation or equivalent
  • Age 18 or above
  • Basic English or Cantonese
  • Interest in professional cooking

CEF Information

The Continuing Education Fund (CEF) provides financial assistance to eligible applicants. For CEF-eligible courses, you may receive up to 80% reimbursement (maximum HK$25,000) upon successful completion.
