Culinary Training Facilities

Professional Training Kitchen

Our main training kitchen features commercial-grade equipment including gas ranges, convection ovens, grills, and specialized stations for different cooking techniques.

  • 20 individual cooking stations
  • Temperature-controlled environment
  • Professional-grade equipment
  • Instructor demonstration area

Specialized Pastry Kitchen

Dedicated pastry and baking facility with specialized equipment for bread making, cake decoration, chocolate work, and advanced pastry techniques.

  • Professional bread ovens
  • Temperature-controlled prep areas
  • Specialized baking equipment
  • Cake decorating stations

Academic Classrooms

Smart Classrooms

Technology-enabled classrooms with interactive whiteboards, projectors, and multimedia capabilities for engaging theoretical instruction.

Learning Resource Center

Well-stocked library with textbooks, industry publications, and digital resources to support independent study and research.
