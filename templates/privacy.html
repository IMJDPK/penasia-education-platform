Privacy Policy

Protecting your personal information is important to us

Last Updated: December 2024

University of PenAsia ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services.

1. Information We Collect

Personal Information

We may collect personal information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Name, email address, phone number, and mailing address
  • Educational background and academic records
  • Employment history and professional qualifications
  • Identity verification documents (HKID, passport)
  • Payment information for course fees
  • Emergency contact information
  • Photos and videos for promotional purposes (with consent)
Automatically Collected Information

When you visit our website, we may automatically collect:

  • IP address and browser information
  • Device type and operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent on our website
  • Referring website or search terms
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

Educational Services
  • Processing applications and enrollment
  • Providing course materials and instruction
  • Tracking academic progress and attendance
  • Issuing certificates and diplomas
  • Providing student support services
Communication
  • Responding to inquiries and providing customer service
  • Sending course updates and important notices
  • Marketing communications (with your consent)
  • Alumni networking and events
Legal and Compliance
  • Complying with Hong Kong education regulations
  • Meeting government reporting requirements
  • Maintaining accreditation standards
  • Protecting against fraud and ensuring security

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We may share your information in the following circumstances:

Government Authorities
  • Education Bureau of Hong Kong for licensing compliance
  • Continuing Education Fund (CEF) administration
  • Immigration Department for student visa matters
  • Other regulatory bodies as required by law
Educational Partners
  • Pearson Education for BTEC qualification verification
  • Partner universities for degree progression
  • Professional bodies for certification purposes
Service Providers
  • Payment processors for fee collection
  • IT service providers for system maintenance
  • Marketing agencies for promotional activities
  • Background check services for student safety

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encryption of sensitive data during transmission
  • Secure servers with restricted access
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Staff training on data protection procedures
  • Backup and disaster recovery systems

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal information for the following periods:

  • Student Records: 7 years after graduation or withdrawal
  • Financial Records: 7 years as required by Hong Kong law
  • Application Materials: 2 years for unsuccessful applications
  • Marketing Data: Until you withdraw consent
  • Website Analytics: 2 years maximum

6. Your Rights

Under Hong Kong's Personal Data (Privacy) Ordinance, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request copies of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured format
  • Withdrawal: Withdraw consent for marketing communications
  • Complaints: Lodge complaints with the Privacy Commissioner

7. Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses cookies to improve your experience:

Essential Cookies
  • Required for website functionality
  • Remember your login status
  • Maintain your application progress
Analytics Cookies
  • Google Analytics for website performance
  • Understanding user behavior patterns
  • Improving website content and navigation
Marketing Cookies
  • Facebook Pixel for targeted advertising
  • Remarketing to website visitors
  • Measuring campaign effectiveness

8. International Students

For international students, we may share information with:

  • Hong Kong Immigration Department for visa processing
  • Your home country's education authorities
  • Embassy or consulate offices
  • Insurance providers for student coverage

9. Third-Party Links

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites. We encourage you to review their privacy policies before providing any information.

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or applicable law. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy on our website
  • Sending email notifications to enrolled students
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
