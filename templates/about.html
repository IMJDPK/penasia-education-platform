About University of Penasia

盈亞大學（University of Penasia - Penasia School of Continuing Education）— 香港領先的商業學府，提供獲國際認可、結合餐飲及酒店管理的專業課程。

With Education Code No. 25291638, we offer professional development opportunities in hospitality, business management, and culinary arts with programs designed to meet industry standards.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality continuing education that empowers individuals to achieve their career goals and contribute meaningfully to Hong Kong's dynamic economy.

Our Vision

To be Hong Kong's leading provider of practical, industry-relevant continuing education programs that bridge the gap between learning and professional success.

Our Values

Excellence in education, practical skills development, student-centered learning, and fostering lifelong learning opportunities for all our students.

Our Institution

University of PenAsia has been serving the Hong Kong community for over 15 years, providing quality business and hospitality education programs that meet the evolving needs of today's workforce. Our institution is fully licensed and recognized by the Education Bureau of Hong Kong.

We are the only institution in Hong Kong providing comprehensive business education with integrated culinary management experience, preparing graduates for leadership roles in the global hospitality industry.

Why Choose University of PenAsia?

  • Accredited courses with quality assurance
  • Industry-experienced instructors
  • Small class sizes for personalized attention
  • Modern facilities and equipment
  • Flexible scheduling options
  • Strong industry connections
  • Comprehensive student support

Our Commitment

We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of education quality and ensuring that our graduates are well-prepared for their chosen career paths. Our programs are regularly updated to reflect current industry trends and employer requirements.
