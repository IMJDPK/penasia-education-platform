## üóëÔ∏è How to Delete a Course

### Important Rules
- ‚ùå **Cannot delete** courses with enrolled students
- ‚ùå **Cannot delete** courses with applications
- ‚úÖ **Can delete** courses with no activity

### Steps
1. Go to **Admin** ‚Üí **Courses**
2. Find the course
3. Click the **trash icon** (Delete button)
4. Confirm deletion
5. If safe: Course is deleted
6. If not safe: Error message suggests deactivation

### Alternative: Deactivate Instead
1. Click **Edit** (pencil icon)
2. Uncheck **"Active"**
3. Click **"Update Course"**
4. Course is hidden but data is preserved

## üí° Tips & Best Practices

### Course Codes
‚úÖ **Good Examples:**
- BUS101, BUS102, BUS201
- CUL101, CUL201
- LANG-ENG-101

‚ùå **Bad Examples:**
- Course1 (not descriptive)
- 101 (too short)
- Business Management 101 (too long, use title field)

### Descriptions
**Include:**
- What students will learn (2-3 sentences)
- Who should take this course (1-2 sentences)
- Why it's valuable (1-2 sentences)
- Any special features (partnerships, facilities, etc.)

**Example:**
```
This comprehensive course introduces students to the fundamentals 
of business management, covering key areas including marketing, 
finance, operations, and strategic planning.

Ideal for aspiring entrepreneurs, business professionals seeking 
formal qualifications, and students planning to pursue business 
degrees.

Taught by industry professionals with real-world experience at 
leading Hong Kong corporations. Includes case studies from 
Peninsula Hotels, Shangri-La, and other industry leaders.
```

### Fees & CEF
- **Regular Fee:** Full price students pay
- **CEF Fee:** Price after government reimbursement
- **CEF Eligible:** Check only if approved by government
- **CEF Fee Calculation:** Usually 80% of regular fee

**Example:**
```
Regular Fee: HKD 15,000
CEF Reimbursement: 80% (HKD 12,000)
CEF Fee: HKD 3,000 (student pays this after reimbursement)
```

### Active vs Featured
| Status | Active | Featured | Result |
|--------|--------|----------|--------|
| New popular course | ‚òë | ‚òë | Visible + Homepage |
| Regular course | ‚òë | ‚òê | Visible only |
| Old/full course | ‚òê | ‚òê | Hidden |
| Testing course | ‚òê | ‚òê | Hidden |

### Student Numbers
- **Min Students:** Class won't run if below this (typically 8-10)
- **Max Students:** Hard limit for quality (typically 20-30)

**Typical Ranges:**
- Lecture courses: 20-40 students
- Practical courses: 12-20 students
- Lab/Hands-on: 8-15 students

## ‚ö†Ô∏è Common Mistakes to Avoid

### ‚ùå Don't Do This:
1. **Using same course code** for different courses
2. **Forgetting to check "Active"** (course won't show!)
3. **Setting fee to 0** for paid courses
4. **Copying course content** from other websites (copyright!)
5. **Deleting courses** with enrollments (use deactivate instead)

### ‚úÖ Do This Instead:
1. Use unique course codes (BUS101, BUS102, etc.)
2. Always check "Active" for new courses
3. Set correct fees in HKD
4. Write original descriptions
5. Deactivate courses instead of deleting

## üÜò Troubleshooting

### Problem: "Course code already exists"
**Solution:** Each course needs a unique code. Try:
- BUS101 ‚Üí BUS102
- CUL-BASIC ‚Üí CUL-BASIC-2

### Problem: "Course not showing on website"
**Solution:** Check these:
1. Is "Active" checked? ‚úì
2. Did you click "Create Course"? ‚úì
3. Did you see success message? ‚úì
4. Try refreshing the courses page

### Problem: "Can't delete course"
**Solution:** Course has enrollments or applications
- Option 1: Deactivate instead (uncheck "Active")
- Option 2: Wait until semester ends
- Option 3: Contact IT admin

### Problem: "CEF fee field not showing"
**Solution:** Check the "CEF Eligible" checkbox first
- The CEF fee field appears automatically when checked

### Problem: "Students seeing wrong fee"
**Solution:** Two fee fields:
- "Course Fee" = What students pay
- "CEF Fee" = After reimbursement (if eligible)
